Volunteering

Volunteering in Coventry

We are the Volunteering & Employability Department and we are here to support Coventry University students and staff to secure rewarding volunteering placements within CUSU and the local community. Our varied volunteering opportunities enable us to offer a stimulating, enjoyable, and educational voluntary experience. Regardless of whether you have loads of spare time every week to fill up, or if you’re just looking at committing an hour or two, we will help you find something that suits your needs.

Volunteering within CUSU is growing and last year we had more than 1500 student volunteers expressing their interest in volunteering. From our side we managed to ensure numerous opportunities for our students, including offering voluntary placements within 130 different organisations across Coventry, Birmingham and Warwickshire. In 2014/2015 we had as many one-off events as ever before, where students could take part in a single volunteering action. Also, if you have your own idea for a volunteering project we can help you to make this a reality and impact upon the local community with our support, contacts, and advice.

Volunteering & Employability (V&E) department is located in The Hub, Room 1.49. Come along and become part of a great team!
