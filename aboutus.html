Student life is not just about studying. It’s about making friends, gaining skills and experience, taking a stand, earning a crust, exploring a new city and getting the right support from the University.

We’re the UK’s largest Students’ Union, providing students with space, resource and money to amplify student life.

But why did we choose to talk about amplification?

Is it because we’ve been one of the best live music venues in Coventry for the last 40 years?

Perhaps it was because we have two brand new, state of the art radio studios broadcasting student radio station Fuse FM?

Maybe it’s because we project the voice of the student back to the University and beyond?
